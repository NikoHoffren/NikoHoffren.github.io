BIOGRAPHY



NIKO HOFFRÉN

Finnish music producer and songwriter Niko Hoffrén has played keyboards from about 5 years old and has been making music since. He started making trance music in 2018. Musical influences are mostly on progressive/power metal genre, bands like Dream Theater and Stratovarius for example.

On electronic music most influencal artists/dj:s have been Armin Van Buuren, Roman Messer and Gareth Emery. Niko has released tracks with labels such as Trancespired Recordings, Sundance Recordings, Liquid Emotion Recordings, Alveda Music, Joyride Music/#WeAreTrance, Neostatics Sounds and Yeiskomp Records.

Niko is also keyboard player of Finnish power metal band Forsaken Gods. Band was exhibited in 2020. He uses Roland JX-305 synthesizer with Arturia KeyLab-49 midi-keyboard and iD4 audio interface.

GEAR

Roland JX-305 GrooveSynth

The Roland JX-305 Groovesynth synthesizer was produced in 1998 by Roland. It is very similar to a Roland MC-505[1] with an additional set of 61 keys. The keyboard is sensitive to velocity and has channel aftertouch. This keyboard was aimed at producers of dance and trance music and was during the late 90s also popular for live performances. The synth has 9 MIDI control knobs and a 2 line LCD display.
