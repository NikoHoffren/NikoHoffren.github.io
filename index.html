BIOGRAPHY

Niko Hoffrén, born in Kuopio, Finland on June, 1986. Niko has played keyboards from about 5 years old and have been making music since. He started making trance music in 2018. Musical influences are mostly on progressive/power metal genre, bands like Dream Theater and Stratovarius for example. On electronic music side most influencal artists/dj:s have been Armin Van Buuren, Roman Messer and Gareth Emery.

Niko is also keyboard player of Finnish power metal band Forsaken Gods. Band was exhibited in 2020. Niko has released tracks with labels such as Trancespired, Sundance, Alveda and Yeiskomp.
